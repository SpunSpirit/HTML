Melissa McCarthy

Life

Melissa Ann McCarthy was born in August 1970 in Plainfield, Illinois. McCarthy was raised on a farm in a large catholic family. Later in Life she would graduate from ST. Francis Academy, and would later attend The Southern Illinois Uni. T hen further down her life she would marry her spouse Ben Falcone; who together are the founders of the On The Day Productions company .Where they both would work together on producing several of the films she started in such as Super Intelligence and Thunder Force.

Early Career

McCarthy's career would start out with her doing stand-up comedy in LA, and later in New York City. She would also perform in New York City as a drag queen going by the name Miss Y. In her Early Career she would appear on tv series such as an appearance on Jenny(When appearing on the show she would be acting opposite her cousin Jenny Mccarthy) and Gilmore Girls. She would also appear in movies such as Drowning Mona, Disneys the Kid, Charlie's Angels, Charlie's Angels full throttle. As She continued to make these film appearances and supporting roles in films she would later on have the leading role on Mike & Molly. Then later on would receive an academy award nomination for Bridesmaids.

Current Day Works

In current day work McCarthy started to lead/co-star in a lot of comedies these movies include The Boss, The Heat, Tammy, Identity Theft, Spy, GhostBusters, Thunder Force, and most recently the little mermaid with her starring as ursula. With the new Little Mermaid movie being her most recent on screen appearance. With her next movie appearance being in Unfrosted: The Pop-Tart Story.
